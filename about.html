Who is Eva Kim?

Eva Kim is a passionate photographer based in Twin Cities, Minnesota. Since 2022, she has been capturing urban architecture, people, beautiful nature of Minnesota and been participating in live events of local communities, to make the most memorable moments freeze in time by a click of her camera.
Whether you want to express yourself in a stylish studio photoshoot, or just need a reliable eye that will highlight the important moments of your special occasion, Eva Kim is here to happily assist you in any journey you are to embark!
